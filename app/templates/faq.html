Frequently Asked Questions

Why am I not receiving any emails?
Please check your spam folder. If you're still not receiving them make sure you put down the correct email address. You can check your current email by clicking on the settings link at the top of the page after you login. If you are still having problems email us at support@equitybuilder.io, in the mean time you can check the current month holdings by logging in.
When will I receive the stock picks?
You will receive the stock picks at the end of the month.
When should I buy these stocks?
To maximize your profit, you should buy the stocks on the first trading day of every month.
Does it matter when I subscribe?
No, it does not.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Login, click on the settings link at the top of the page, click on subscription on the sidebar, then click on the red cancel button. Or click here.
