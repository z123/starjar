About the strategy

Overview

  • The strategy trades between 9 sector ETFs and 2 bond ETFs.
  • Annual return of 11%.
  • Max drawdown of only 14%.
  • Is always invested in bonds, so your funds will be safe even if the market has a serious downturn.
  • Trades monthly, so following the strategy is very simple.

Our strategy uses a combination of relative momentum, mean reversion, along with current market volatility to calculate what positions to buy. Traditionally the S&P 500 (the S&P 500 is the top 500 companies currently on the US market) has been a very hard index to beat. Our strategy identifies sectors within the S&P 500 that are doing well and invests in those sectors, leaving out the bad parts. Subscribers to our strategy will receive positions on the last day of the month, and should place the trades at the market open of the following trading day.

The graph above shows the performance of our strategy compared to the just holding S&P 500 . We can see that our strategy not only out performs the S&P 500 but also has better downside protection.

Across all metrics, the strategy has been a significant improvement over buying and holding the S&P 500, but most importantly, the strategy has done very well managing portfolio drawdowns (losses).

A “drawdown curve” shows the percentage a portfolio was down at any given point relative to the portfolio’s previous peak value. A reading of -10% would indicate the portfolio was down 10% from its previous peak. A reading of 0% would indicate the portfolio hit an all time high on that day.

Note how the strategy significantly protects the portfolio from even the most severe market downturns.
