About the strategy

Overview

  • The strategy trades between 9 sector etfs and 2 bond etfs.
  • Annual return of 11%.
  • Max drawdown of only 13%.
  • Is always invested in bonds, so your funds will be safe even if the market has a serious downturn.
  • Trades monthly, so following the strategy is very simple.

Our strategy uses a combination of relative momentum, mean reversion, along with current market volitality to calculate what positions to buy. Subscribers to our strategy will recieve positions on the last trading day of the month, and should place the trades at the market open of the following trading day.

The graph above shows the performance of our strategy compared to the just holding S&P 500 (the S&P 500 is the top 500 companies currently on the US market). We can see that our strategy not only performs better but also has better downside protection.
